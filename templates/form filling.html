How to Fill RTI Application

Step 1: Applicant Details

Fill in your complete name, address, and contact information. Ensure your mobile number and email are correct for communication.

Step 2: Public Authority

Mention the complete name and address of the government department or public authority you're addressing the RTI to.

Step 3: Information Sought

Clearly specify the information you require. Be precise and mention relevant time periods if applicable. You can ask multiple questions but keep them numbered.

Step 4: Payment Details

RTI applications typically require a fee of ₹10. Mention how you've paid (IPO, demand draft, court fee stamp, etc.) and include proof.

Step 5: Submit Application

Sign and date the application. Keep a copy for your records before submitting to the Public Information Officer of the concerned department.
